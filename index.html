How MedLink TX works
3 quick steps to use this directory
This isn’t a replacement for a doctor or counselor. It’s a clean, simple starting point to help people in and around Allen find real-world resources.
Scroll down to the cards below and click “Call” or “Visit site” to contact each place directly.
Step 1

Pick what you need help with

Decide if you’re looking for medical care (checkups, chronic illness, allergies), mental health care (therapy, teen programs), or a crisis hotline. The filters and search bar can help you narrow down.

Step 2

Scan the cards for cost + location

Cards labeled Free / sliding-scale are meant for uninsured or low-income residents. Others may take insurance or have regular visit fees. Double-check details with the clinic when you call.

Step 3

Call, ask questions, and confirm

When you call, ask about cost, what services they offer, and whether they are accepting new patients. Every situation is different, and the clinic is the best source for up-to-date info.
