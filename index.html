Why this project exists
MedLink TX began as a high school project to bring local health and mental health resources together on one clear, calm page.

Finding help can feel confusing when you are juggling search results, insurance questions, and out-of-date websites. This site focuses on reliable starting points for people in Allen and Collin County: free and sliding-scale clinics, counseling resources for youth and families, and crisis hotlines that are available 24/7. Every listing links back to its official organization so you can double-check details before you go.
