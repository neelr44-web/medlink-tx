About this project
MedLink TX began as a high school student project to make it easier for people in our area to find health support without worrying about cost.

Disclaimer: This website is for general information only and is not a substitute for professional medical or mental health advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Information may change, so please confirm details with each organization directly.
